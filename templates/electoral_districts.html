Analysis by Electoral Districts

It can be seen that geographically, the electoral districts become smaller for highly populated provinces like Ontario and Alberta. For sparsely populated provinces and territories, the electoral districts tend to be bigger.For example,for Nunavut, the whole province is one big electoral district. However, most of the electoral districts are valued quite close to 100%. Conclusion: Overall the representation is fair, but a slight shift of Federal Electoral District distribution to improve the representation of Alberta and reduce the representation of Prince Edward Island is advisable.
